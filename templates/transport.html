Transport

  • A fleet of 70 buses is available for the faculty and students for commuting between campus and their place of residence.
  • College buses ply in various routes to accommodate the needs of the students and the staff from different corners of West Godavari district.
  • Free transportation is provided for the faculty coming from Tadepalligudem.
  • 50% concession in bus fee is given to the faculty travelling from places other than Tadepalligudem.
  • Transport facility is provided to the students to attend pool campus drives organized in other colleges.
