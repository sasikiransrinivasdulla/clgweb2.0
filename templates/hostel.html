About Us

EAPCET/ECET/ICET/PGCET/POLYCET code: VSVT

Hostel Haven
The hostel facilities at Sri Vasavi Engineering College are designed to provide a comfortable and conducive environment for students.The hostel experience is aimed at fostering a sense of community and helping students maintain a balanced lifestyle while pursuing their academic goals.

Gents Hostel

The gents hostel in SVEC provides spacious rooms with basic amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay for students. It features 24/7 security, well-maintained common areas, and access to study rooms. The hostel fosters a disciplined yet friendly environment, perfect for academic focus and social interactions.

Hostel No.1:

  • Intake capacity: 250
  • Allocation: Diploma, B.Tech II, III, IV Years

Hostel No.2:

  • Intake capacity: 250
  • Allocation: B.Tech year, M.tech, MCA, MBA

Ladies Hostel

The ladies hostel in SVEC offers a safe and peaceful atmosphere for female students, with well-ventilated rooms and essential facilities. It emphasizes security, with round-the-clock surveillance and a supportive community for all residents. The hostel provides a comfortable space for both study and relaxation.

Hostel No.1:

  • Intake capacity: 250
  • Allocation: Diploma, B.Tech II, III, IV Years

Hostel No.2:

  • Intake capacity: 250
  • Allocation: B.Tech year, M.tech, MCA, MBA

Hostel Facilities

Spacious Rooms

Well-ventilated and comfortable rooms with all necessary furnishings. Ideal for studying and relaxing.

Hygienic Mess

Healthy and nutritious food with multiple meal options every day. Fresh ingredients for every meal.

24/7 Security

Round-the-clock security with CCTV surveillance to ensure the safety of all students in hostel premises.

High-Speed Wi-Fi

High-speed internet connectivity available in all hostel areas. Stay connected anytime!

Recreational Facilities

Indoor games, TV lounges, and outdoor sports to unwind and relax after a busy day.

Hostel Rules & Regulations

  • 1. Check-In Time: 2:00 PM onwards.
  • 2. Check-Out Time: 7:30 PM.
  • 3. Cleanliness: Students must keep their area clean and dispose of trash in designated bins.
  • 4. Security: Always lock your door and keep valuables in the provided lockers.
  • 5. Noise: Avoid loud noise or music that can disturb other residents.
  • 6. Ragging: Ragging in any form in the hostel is illegal. Sent to jail range from 6 months to 10 years in jail, with fines up to Rs. 50,000/-.
